# THE MODULES

Below are the eight HR sub-modules plus the Payroll engine. Each block gives a short
"what it is" line, a feature bullet list, and a client-facing benefit.

---

## 1. Core HR & Employee Records 👤
**The single source of truth for every employee.**

- Complete employee master file: identity, contact, personal, and employment details
- Organization structure: **Departments** and **Designations** with parent/child hierarchy
- Reporting lines — assign a manager and see every direct report
- Pay grade linkage and basic salary on each profile
- Emergency contacts, employee documents, and contracts attached to each person
- Localized for Bangladesh: National ID, Birth Certificate, Passport, **TIN**, division/district,
  religion, blood group, nationality
- Employment types: **Permanent · Contract · Daily**
- Lifecycle status: **Active · Inactive · Terminated**
- Contract management: Permanent, Fixed-term, Probation, Internship, Consultant

**Benefit:** *No more scattered spreadsheets — every detail about every employee, always
up to date and instantly searchable.*

---

## 2. Attendance & Time Tracking ⏱️
**Know who's in, who's out, and who's owed overtime.**

- Daily attendance logs with check-in / check-out and automatic work-hour calculation
- Overtime hours captured per day
- Multiple capture sources: **Biometric · Manual · Geo-Fence · Mobile**
- Attendance states: Present, Absent, Half-Day, Late, Work-From-Home, On-Leave, Holiday, Week-Off
- **Work schedules** with start/end times, flexible-hours option, grace period, and working-days-per-week
- **Shifts** (including night shifts) and employee rostering
- Manual edit tracking — every correction is flagged and attributed

**Benefit:** *Accurate, tamper-evident attendance that flows straight into payroll.*

---

## 3. Leave Management 🌴
**Transparent leave, automatic balances, multi-step approvals.**

- Configurable **leave types** (annual, sick, maternity, etc.) with full policy controls:
  - Days per year, paid vs. unpaid, carry-forward with caps
  - Minimum notice period, maximum consecutive days
  - Document-required flag (e.g., medical certificate)
  - Gender-restricted leave (e.g., maternity/paternity)
- Live **leave balances** per employee per year — allocated, used, pending, carried-forward, remaining
- **Multi-level approval workflow**: Manager → HR → Approved, with rejection reasons
- Request states: Draft, Pending, Approved-by-Manager, Approved-by-HR, Approved, Rejected, Cancelled, Revoked

**Benefit:** *Employees always know their balance; managers approve in a click; HR keeps policy airtight.*

---

## 4. Payroll Engine 💰
**Run accurate monthly payroll with confidence.**

- **Salary Grades** — define pay bands (min/max) and the basic-salary percentage split
- **Salary Components** — build allowances and deductions:
  - Allowances: House Rent, Medical, Transport, Food, Other
  - Deductions: Provident Fund, Tax, Loan, Advance, Other
  - Calculation by **Fixed amount, % of Basic, or % of Gross**; taxable/non-taxable flags
- **Employee salary structures** — assign each employee a grade, gross, basic, and component breakdown
- **Monthly payroll cycles** — one run per month with start/end dates and full status workflow
  (Draft → Processing → Processed → Approved → Paid)
- **Per-employee payroll entries / payslips** capturing gross, basic, allowances, deductions, tax,
  overtime pay, bonus, and net salary — plus working/present/absent/leave days
- **Overtime rules** — configurable multipliers (e.g., 1.5×), monthly caps, weekend handling
- **Expense claims** — employees submit expenses (Travel, Food, Medical, Fuel, etc.) with receipts and
  an approval/reimbursement workflow
- Cycle totals roll up automatically: total basic, allowances, deductions, net pay, and headcount

**Benefit:** *What used to take days of spreadsheet work becomes a controlled, reviewable
monthly process — with a payslip for every employee.*

---

## 5. Performance Management 🎯
**Goals, KPIs, and 360° reviews that actually get done.**

- **Review cycles** (e.g., quarterly, annual) to organize evaluations
- Review types: **Self · Manager · Peer · Subordinate · HR 360°**
- Overall ratings, strengths, areas-for-improvement, and reviewer comments
- **Goals** with target vs. achieved values, weighting, status, and due dates
- **KPI library** — reusable measures linked to departments or designations
- Feedback requests and review status tracking (Draft → Submitted → Acknowledged)

**Benefit:** *Structured, fair evaluations that link individual goals to company KPIs.*

---

## 6. Recruitment (Applicant Tracking) 🧲
**From job post to hire, all tracked.**

- **Job postings** with auto-generated job codes, department/designation, requirements,
  experience range, salary range, and required headcount
- Posting status: Draft, Published, Closed, On-Hold
- **Candidate database** with source tracking (LinkedIn, Referral, Job Portal, Direct)
- **Applications** linking candidates to postings, with status pipeline (Shortlisted, Rejected, etc.)
- **Interview scheduling** — Phone Screen, Video Call, Technical Test, HR, Panel, Final

**Benefit:** *A simple ATS that keeps every candidate and interview organized in one pipeline.*

---

## 7. Onboarding ✅
**Give every new hire a structured first week.**

- Reusable **onboarding checklists** built from task templates
- Per-employee onboarding with task-by-task progress tracking
- Status from assignment through completion

**Benefit:** *Consistent, repeatable onboarding so nothing falls through the cracks.*

---

## 8. Training & Development 📚
**Plan, deliver, and track employee training.**

- **Training programs** with category, mode (Online / In-Person / Blended), provider, duration,
  cost, capacity, and schedule
- Certificate tracking
- Employee enrolment and training status (Upcoming, Ongoing, etc.)

**Benefit:** *Invest in your people and prove it — every program and participant on record.*

---

## 9. Asset Management 💻
**Track company assets handed to employees.**

- **Asset register**: laptops, desktops, mobiles, vehicles, furniture, measuring tools,
  safety equipment, and more
- Brand, model, serial number, purchase value, condition, and location
- **Assignment** of assets to employees, with status (Available, Assigned, etc.)

**Benefit:** *Always know what equipment is out, with whom, and in what condition.*

# 🟦 WHY uOrgHub (cross-cutting strengths — great for a "Built-in by design" panel)

- **🔒 Role-based security** — granular, claim-based permissions control who can view, create,
  edit, export, approve, and delete in every module.
- **🧾 Full audit trail** — records are never hard-deleted; every entry keeps created/updated/deleted
  history for compliance and accountability.
- **🔁 Multi-level approval workflows** — leave and expenses route through manager and HR sign-off.
- **📤 One-click export** — every list exports to **Excel (XLSX) or CSV** for reporting and sharing.
- **🔎 Instant search & sorting** — fast, paginated lists across thousands of records.
- **🇧🇩 Localized** — Bangladesh-ready fields (NID, TIN, divisions, districts, religion, blood group)
  and BDT-friendly money handling.
- **🌐 Modern web app** — responsive, browser-based, no installation for end users.
- **🧩 Part of a full ERP** — HR & Payroll connect seamlessly with Accounts, Inventory,
  Procurement, and Projects in the same uOrgHub platform.
